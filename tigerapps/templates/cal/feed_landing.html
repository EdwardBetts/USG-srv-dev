To subscribe to the Live Feed above, select a calendar option below for instructions:

Google Calendar
  1. Copy the full webcal:// address from the blue box above
  2. Open your Google Calendar
  3. Under 'Other calendars' click 'Add' and then 'Add by URL'
  4. Paste the address from above and click 'Add calendar'
Apple iCal
  1. Copy the full webcal:// address from the blue box above
  2. Open iCal on your computer
  3. From the 'Calendar' menu, click 'Subscribe...'
  4. Paste the address from above and click 'Subscribe'
  5. Set the 'Auto-refresh' option to 'Every hour' for best results
  6. Click "OK"
Microsoft Outlook
  • Copy the full webcal:// address from the blue box above
  • Open your 'Account Settings' window in Microsoft Outlook
  • Select the 'Internet Calendars' tab
  • Click 'New...' and the paste the address into the window
  • Click 'Add'
