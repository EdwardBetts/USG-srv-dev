{% comment %} Sync with Google, iCal, Outlook {% endcomment %}

To subscribe to the Live Feed above, select a calendar option below for instructions:

Google Calendar
  1. Copy the full webcal:// address from the blue box above
  2. Open your Google Calendar
  3. Under 'Other calendars' in the left panel, click 'Add' and then 'Add by URL'
  4. Paste the address from above and click 'Add calendar'
  • Note: Google Calendar only syncs once every 24 hours. Events you add on the Student Events Calendar may experience up to a 24 hour delay. To avoid this delay, use the "Add to Google Calendar" button next to each event instead.
Apple iCal
  1. Copy the full webcal:// address from the blue box above
  2. Open iCal on your computer
  3. From the 'Calendar' menu, click 'Subscribe...'
  4. Paste the address from above and click 'Subscribe'
  5. Set the 'Auto-refresh' option to 'Every hour' for best results
  6. Click "OK"
Microsoft Outlook
  1. Copy the full webcal:// address from the blue box above
  2. Open your 'Account Settings' window in Microsoft Outlook
  3. Select the 'Internet Calendars' tab
  4. Click 'New...' and the paste the address into the window
  5. Click 'Add'
