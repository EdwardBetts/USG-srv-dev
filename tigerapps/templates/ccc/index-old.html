What it is

The Campuswide Community Challenge is a collective effort to try and reach 10,000 hours of community service. Besides a good conscience, teams or individuals can win prizes for being a good samaritan.

How it works

Sign up as a team or as an individual and log your hours. Awards will be given to teams with most hours per individual, individuals with the most hours and to the coolest single volunteer project.

Prizes

Prizes include one million dollars, a better room draw and a candlelight dinner with USG VP Catherine Etteman! Also, everyone gets a pretty snazzy free t-shirt.
