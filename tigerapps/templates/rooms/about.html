Floor Plan Navigation


Locate rooms and facilities quickly, in a simple, visual way.

Click a building pin on the map to see floorplans. Click a room on the floorplan image to see its details.

Real-time Collaborative Queue


Manage your preferred rooms with friends. Add or remove rooms, drag and drop to reorder, and immediately see any changes made by your room draw group members.

Searching


Quickly locate rooms by searching and sorting over a number of attributes, like building name, room size, and floor number.

Reviews


Read opinions from other students to find the best rooms, and help future residents by reviewing the rooms you've lived in.

Disclaimer
