1. Interface

The TigerAlbum interface is centered around the map of Princeton and is simple and intuitive to use! You can interact with the map just the way you interact with Google Maps -- scrolling corresponds to zooming in/out of the map. Zooming can also be controlled using the zoom slider on the side. You can drag the map using the hand-shaped cursor in order to change which part of the map you are viewing at a given time.

Recently submitted/popular photos are posted on the map as thumbnails at the respective locations they were posted from. You can click on these to view them in normal size, view how popular they are in terms of likes and view comments associated with them. You can also comment on or like a photo yourself. Right underneath the photo, you are given a permalink in case you want to share it with friends. You are also given a report link in case you find the photo offensive/disturbing and wish to bring this to the moderators' attention. Finally, closing the photo box brings you back to the map you were originally looking at.

2. Submission

You can submit a photograph by clicking on the submit button on the upper right corner of the TigerAlbum home page. This will redirect you to a submission page where you can upload a photograph and caption it if you so desire. Please note that at this point, we are only able to accept JPEG files, so all other formats will be rejected. Once you have successfully uploaded a photo, click on the submit photo button to proceed.

The next step to your submission is associating your photo with a location. If your photo had geotagged data associated with it (for instance, if you had taken the photograph using a smartphone), TigerAlbum does the best it can to place your photo on the map at the appropriate location. You are still free to place it somewhere else if you think we got it wrong, before you make your final move to submit. If however we could not find any location data off your photo, you will need to click on the map to place your photo at a certain location before you submit.

Once you submit, you will be taken back to the homepage. Don't be sad if you can't spot your photo right away! You must wait for it to be moderated before it shows up on the map. Your photo should show up within a day, and you're good to go!

3. Frequently asked questions

I submitted my photo correctly, why isn't it showing up on the map?
All submissions are moderated. Give us time, we usually approve photos within a day. And thanks for submitting, we think you're awesome.
Can I submit photos outside Princeton's campus boundaries?
For now, we're only accepting photo submissions taken on and near the campus. We're hoping to expand out in the future, however!
Can I upload a bunch of photos to TigerAlbum all at once?
In order to prevent spam, we limit you to a maximum of 3 photo uploads every 10 minutes. Sorry for any inconvenience this may cause!
